Fundraising

What if we tell you that you can turn

your passion and compassion into

a bigger platform?

Small Actions x Lots of People = Big Change

Orphan Life Foundation strives to provide helpless children with basic necessities, education, and reliable health services.

But without your support and donations, we wouldn't be able to do what we do today!

Yes, with the fundraising option, you can convince more people to participate in the cause of meeting the needs of the orphans!

Interested in Fundraising?

Follow these guideliness

If you plan to raise money in support of Orphan Life Foundation, we want to make sure you do so in a legal and secure way. Please read the following rules before you begin:

Orphan Life Foundation's charity number is 81-4314076. Please display this number on any fundraising material you use and hand out to participants and the general audience.

Any fundraising material you may use MUST NOT suggest that you represent Orphan Life Foundation. It should CLEARLY STATE that you are only organizing a fundraiser in support of Orphan Life Foundation.

We advise you not to collect money in a public place or by going door-to-door. If you do choose to do so, you'll need a personal license from your local authority (you should find details regarding this on your local authority's website).

If children under the age of 16 are involved in the fundraising event in any capacity, please make sure they have permission from their parents/guardian and are properly supervised by a responsible adult during the entire course of the event. Children should be discouraged from approaching strangers regarding fundraising.

If you are planning to have any type of lottery (such as a raffle or sweepstake) section in the fundraiser, please approach us before doing so as there are strict rules about what you can and cannot do.

If you do something that threatens or damages the name or reputation of Orphan Life Foundation, we reserve the right to ask you to stop fundraising immediately.

Please encourage eligible donors/sponsors to make aid declarations before donating money. This will enable us to recover the tax applicable to their donations.

We would like to receive photographs of your fundraising efforts. If you wish to share these with us, please email them toinfo@orphanlifefoundation.org. We will use them to promote our work.

Fundraising Ideas

1

Host a Dinner

What could possibly be a better fundraising idea than to gather all your friends and family for a heart-warming dinner?

You can ask the guests for admission fees or to make things interesting, ask everyone to bring in their own dishes and auction them!

2

Donate Presents

Parents often like donating the extra toys, clothes, and books of their children to the less privileged. Do you do the same?

If yes, consider asking your family and friends to donate instead of sending presents this holiday season!

This is an amazingly simple fundraising idea that will fill your hearts with gratitude and contentment.

3

Garage Sale

What about finding things that are extra in your home and putting them up for a garage sale?

You can donate a portion of or better yet, all of your garage sale profits to the orphans!

On the side, it will help you clean up your home and make space for new things - a win-win for everyone!

Use Smile Amazon to Automatically Donate to Orphan Life Foundation

If you frequently shop at Amazon, you can simply sign up and use Smile.Amazon.com to automatically donate to Orphan Life Foundation by choosing us as the preferred charity.

Amazon donates 0.5% of the price of the products that are Eligible for Amazon Smile Donation.

From order placement to shipping, the entire shopping experience is the same at Smile.Amazon as Amazon - you'll just be donating an amount for the orphans in need.

Remember, every penny counts!
